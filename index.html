Benefits of Computing in Society

Author: Andy



In the long history of human civilization, it is a few momentus occasions that pushed and advanced human development. The agricultural revolution marked the transition away from a hunting and gathering lifestyle and made large-scale human settlement possible. The industrial revolution reforms how we manufacture and produce, giving rise to unprecedented productivity and efficiency. And now, the revolution of computing and digitalisation may be the next chapter of the ever continuing progress of human endeavour and advancement.
