Three facts of smartphones

1. The first smartphone

The first smartphone is actually not Apple's Iphone. In fact, the first smartphone was manufactured by IBM in 1994 and it's called Simon Person Cpmmunicator. It already had touchscreen on it, and it could also receive faxes and emails. Futhermore, it also included an address book, canlenda, appointment scheduler, calculator, world time clock, notepad, maps, stock reports...etc.
